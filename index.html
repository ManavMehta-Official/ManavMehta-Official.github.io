About
Me.

I know my name is all over the website but still, just in case you did not read it, my name is Manav Mehta! I'm a 15-year-old currently studying in 10th grade at Delhi Public School, Bangalore. I feel happy to be a part of this great school with good friends, helpful and loving teachers and sound school administration. I am good at a lot of things ranging from studies to sports. My favourite subject is Biology and my favourite sport is football and swimming.

Since childhood, I developed an interest in the field of computer science. I wanted to understand how these fascinating machines operated. I started reading books, blogs, articles etc. to grasp as much as knowledge possible on the topic. Thus, my ambition became to excel in the field of computer sciences. I started attending various courses on computer science but one course that stood out among all was "cs50: introduction to computer science." This course has been a major part of the development of my programming skills.

These are all the things which express me. Though nobody can be described in a few sets of sentences. One needs to have yet command of oneself before going to write something about his life. Life is meant to be lived avidly and with visualization to do good for your fellow beings. Keeping this aim in mind, I have always desired to serve my people in whatever capacity I can.
